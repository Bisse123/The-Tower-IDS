Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the "The Tower ImportData" Google Apps Script ("the App") accesses, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Access

  • The App only accesses Google Sheets that you explicitly have linked in your IDS Master's Google Sheet.
  • The App does not collect or store any personal data outside your Google account.

How We Use Your Data

  • The App reads and writes data to your Google Sheets as part of its data import/export functionality.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Data Storage

  • The App does not store your data on any external servers.
  • All processing is done within your Google Account and Google's infrastructure.

Data Sharing

  • The App does not share your data with any third parties.

Security

  • The App uses Google's secure authentication and authorization protocols.
  • Only users with explicit access to your Google Sheets can use the App.

Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here.
